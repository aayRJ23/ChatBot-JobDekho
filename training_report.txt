Q: How do I apply for a job?
A: Open the job listing you want and click the Apply Now button. Fill in your name email phone number address cover letter and upload your resume then click Send Application.

Q: I forgot my password
A: Click on the Forgot Password link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q: How do I post a job?
A: You must be logged in as an Employer. Click POST NEW JOB in the sidebar fill in the title category country city location salary type salary amount and description then click Create Job.

Q: What is the Interview Dashboard?
A: The Interview Dashboard shows all accepted applications with scheduled interviews. Job seekers can see their interview schedule and final result. Employers can see all candidates and set the final verdict.

Q: How do real time notifications work?
A: The app uses Socket.IO so you receive notifications instantly without needing to refresh. As long as you are logged in updates arrive automatically.

Q: How do I accept an application?
A: On the My Applications page click Accept and Schedule Interview on the applicant card. Fill in the interview date time and meeting link then confirm.

Q: What job categories are available?
A: The available categories are Graphics and Design Mobile App Development Frontend Web Development MERN Stack Development Account and Finance Artificial Intelligence Video Animation MEAN Stack Development MEVN Stack Development and Data Entry Operator.
